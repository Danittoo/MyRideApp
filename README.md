# MyRideApp
This is an app created for people to book a ride hassle-free. Problem solvein app for the inability to find ride partners to partner with for a journey to and fro a city. Solution: An App that pairs riders going to the same destination to help curb the issues of looking for a driver or asking for someone to help out. The app would be solving the issue of finding or sourcing out a driver or ride willing to pair with the individual. It would pair two or more people having a common destination, give estimated or exact price for the trip, also pair people needing a future ride in advance. areas within and outside the city.
